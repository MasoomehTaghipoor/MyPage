Welcome to my page

I am a Research Engineer and Deputy Head of the MOSAR team at INRAE/AgroParisTech/Paris Saclay. My main research interests include using and developing mathematical models to extract insights from precision livestock farming data. I am particularly interested in characterizing animal responses to environmental challenges and studying their impact on welfare.

Since 2022, I have been co-leading the national project WAIT4, focused on "Characterization of animal activities and social interactions generating positive or negative emotions, using artificial intelligence."

Strongly convinced of the need to educate interdisciplinary scientists in animal science, I supervise MSc and PhD projects at the intersection of animal science, model development, and data analysis.

Ce site présente mon parcours, mes recherches et mes activités d’enseignement.
